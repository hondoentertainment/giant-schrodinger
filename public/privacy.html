Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-16

What we collect

Venn with Friends is designed to minimize data collection. By default, gameplay stats (scores, streaks, submissions) are stored only on your device via browser localStorage. If you opt in to multiplayer, limited gameplay data is sent to our backend so other players can see your rounds. Anonymous analytics events (round starts, screen views) may be recorded when enabled. Error reports may be captured if error monitoring is enabled, including the URL, browser version, and stack traces.

What we don't collect

We do not collect your name, email, phone number, address, or any other personal identifier unless you provide it. We do not collect payment information (unless and until a shop is enabled). We do not embed third-party advertising or tracking pixels.

Third parties

  • Google Gemini — when AI scoring is active, your submission text and concept labels are sent to Google's Generative Language API for scoring and optional image generation.
  • Supabase — when multiplayer is enabled, room state, player handles, and submissions are stored in our Supabase backend.
  • Sentry — when error monitoring is enabled, uncaught errors and stack traces are sent to Sentry for debugging.

Your choices

You can clear all locally stored data at any time by opening your browser's developer tools, going to the Application (or Storage) tab, and clearing localStorage for this site. You can also decline multiplayer and play entirely offline.
